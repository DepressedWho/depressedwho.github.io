Our Team

We're a diverse group of Gen Z individuals who've walked through the darkness and found our way back to the light. Each of us has a unique story, but we share one common goal: to help others realize they're not alone in their struggles.

Our Mission

To create a judgment-free, accessible mental health support community where Gen Z can find understanding, hope, and practical help through peer support. We believe that sometimes the best help comes from someone who's been exactly where you are.
